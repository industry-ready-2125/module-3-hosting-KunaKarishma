What is WhatsApp?

WhatsApp is a messaging application that allows users to send text messages, voice messages, make voice and video calls, share images, documents, user locations, and other media.

Key Features of WhatsApp

  • Instant Messaging
  • Voice and Video Calls
  • Group Chat
  • End-to-End Encryption
  • Media Sharing
  • Status Updates
  • WhatsApp Web
  • WhatsApp Business

History of WhatsApp

WhatsApp was founded in 2009 by Brian Acton and Jan Koum. It was later acquired by Facebook in 2014. Since its inception, WhatsApp has grown to become one of the most popular messaging apps globally, with billions of users worldwide.
